Who We Are

Empowering francophone Africa through knowledge and skill.

Mandatheque is born out of a desire to share knowledge from it’s founder and CEO, Theophane NGBABA, a social entrepreneur who believes as Mandela Nelson puts it, that “Education is the most powerful weapon to bring about change”. Founded in 2017, Mandatheque intents to build a community of learners sharing and receiving innovative contents using technology in order to provide an easy and affordable access to knowledge to everyone.
