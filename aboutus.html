About Us

We offer modern solutions to growing your knowledge & Skills

Mandatheque is a virtual learning platform for learners including students, professional adults and the general public who want to read, learn & explore innovative contents.

At Mandatheque we focus on user learning experience to deliver innovative and practical contents created based on actual needs of the African specially and the global learning sphere at large. Thanks to our customer-centric approach our work starts from need assessment and continued monitoring through documenting recommendations from users in order to ensure a strong emphasis on results and customer satisfaction

Who we are

Mandatheque is born out of a desire to share knowledge from it’s founder and CEO, Theophane NGBABA, a social entrepreneur who believes as Mandadela Nelson puts it, that “Education is the most powerful weapon to bring about change”. Founded in 2017, Mandatheque entents to build a community of learners sharing and receiving innovative contents using technology in order to provide an easy and affordable access to knowledge to everyone.

In order to bring about this dream and feed our platform with content we work in collaboration with Government, institutions, independent content creators, teachers, experts on variety of thematic including Digital skills, Agri-business/agriculture, Business & Entrepreneurship, Academics, Language and translation services, Ebook, Press and many more.

In that line we provide space for both learners to get knowledge and opportunities as well as for content creators to live from their work using technology.

Mandatheque objective is provide equal chance of education to the most vulnerable population specially african schools and learners who lack opportunities. With many talents we see in Africa, there is a real lack of opportunities to learn in order to change the narrative.

Vision

Mandatheque’s vision is to be a reference in the industry of e-learning, recognized for its user experience-based approach in delivering practical and innovative contents that fit the current needs of the African society.

Mission

Our Mission is to create a virtual space, safe and convenient for learners by merging technology and resources from various sources in order to enhance efforts in increasing literacy rate in Africa.
